HIIT Fat Burn Workout

20-30 minutes • Maximum calorie burn

Workout Structure

Frequency: 4-5 days per week

Work-Rest Ratio: 30s work, 15s rest

Equipment: None required

Calorie Burn: 300-500 calories per session

Circuit 1 - Full Body Burn

Repeat 3 rounds

  • ✅ Burpees - 30 seconds
  • ✅ Mountain Climbers - 30 seconds
  • ✅ Jump Squats - 30 seconds
  • ✅ High Knees - 30 seconds
  • ✅ Rest - 60 seconds between rounds

Circuit 2 - Core Focus

Repeat 3 rounds

  • ✅ Plank Jacks - 30 seconds
  • ✅ Russian Twists - 30 seconds
  • ✅ Bicycle Crunches - 30 seconds
  • ✅ Leg Raises - 30 seconds
  • ✅ Rest - 45 seconds between rounds

Circuit 3 - Cardio Blast

Repeat 4 rounds

  • ✅ Jumping Jacks - 30 seconds
  • ✅ Butt Kicks - 30 seconds
  • ✅ Skaters - 30 seconds
  • ✅ Power Knees - 30 seconds
  • ✅ Rest - 30 seconds between rounds

Weekly Schedule

  • Monday: Circuit 1 + Circuit 2
  • Tuesday: Circuit 3 + Core Focus
  • Wednesday: Active Recovery (Walking/Yoga)
  • Thursday: Circuit 1 + Circuit 3
  • Friday: Circuit 2 + Cardio Blast
  • Saturday: Full Body HIIT (All circuits)
  • Sunday: Rest
