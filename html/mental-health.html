Mental Health & Wellness

Tools and techniques for stress management, mindfulness and better mental health

Stress Management Techniques

Deep Breathing

Calm your nervous system instantly

  • Sit comfortably with straight back
  • Inhale deeply through nose for 4 counts
  • Hold breath for 4 counts
  • Exhale slowly through mouth for 6 counts
  • Repeat 5-10 times

Progressive Muscle Relaxation

Release physical tension

  • Start from toes and move upward
  • Tense each muscle group for 5 seconds
  • Release and relax for 30 seconds
  • Notice the difference in sensation
  • Complete full body in 15 minutes

5-4-3-2-1 Grounding

Stay present during anxiety

  • Name 5 things you can see
  • Name 4 things you can touch
  • Name 3 things you can hear
  • Name 2 things you can smell
  • Name 1 thing you can taste

Mindfulness Practices

Daily Mindfulness

  1. Start with 5 minutes of quiet sitting
  2. Focus on your breath without changing it
  3. When mind wanders, gently bring it back
  4. Practice while doing daily activities
  5. Eat one meal mindfully each day
  6. Notice sensations without judgment

Gratitude Practice

  1. Keep a gratitude journal
  2. Write 3 things you're grateful for daily
  3. Be specific about why you're grateful
  4. Include small everyday things
  5. Share gratitude with others
  6. Notice how it changes your perspective

Better Sleep Tips

Consistent Schedule

Sleep and wake at same time daily

Digital Detox

No screens 1 hour before bedtime

Cool Room

Keep bedroom temperature around 18-20°C

Relaxing Routine

Develop pre-sleep calming activities
