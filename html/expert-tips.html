Dr. Priya Sharma

Hydration is Key

Drink at least 2-3 liters of water daily. Proper hydration improves metabolism, energy levels, and overall health. Add lemon or mint for flavor.

Rajesh Kumar
Fitness Trainer

Consistency Over Intensity

Regular moderate exercise is better than occasional intense workouts. Aim for 30 minutes daily rather than 3 hours once a week.

Dr. Anjali Mehta
Psychologist

Mindful Eating

Eat without distractions. Focus on your food, chew properly, and listen to your body's hunger signals. This improves digestion and prevents overeating.
