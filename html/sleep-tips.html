Sleep Tips

Better sleep techniques for improved health and wellness

Sleep Improvement Techniques

Consistent Schedule

  • Sleep and wake at same time daily
  • Even on weekends maintain routine
  • Set bedtime alarm reminder
  • Allow 7-9 hours for quality sleep

Sleep Environment

  • Keep room temperature 18-20°C
  • Use blackout curtains for darkness
  • Reduce noise with earplugs
  • Comfortable mattress and pillows

Digital Detox

  • No screens 1 hour before bed
  • Use blue light filters in evening
  • Charge phone outside bedroom
  • Read physical books instead

Bedtime Routine

  • Warm bath or shower before bed
  • Light stretching or gentle yoga
  • Meditation or deep breathing
  • Calming herbal tea

Sleep Nutrition

  • Avoid heavy meals before bedtime
  • Limit caffeine after 2 PM
  • Light snack if hungry at night
  • Foods rich in tryptophan

Daytime Habits

  • Morning sunlight exposure
  • Regular exercise during day
  • Limit daytime napping
  • Manage stress throughout day
