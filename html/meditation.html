Types of Meditation

Mindfulness Meditation

Focus on present moment awareness without judgment. Perfect for beginners to develop concentration.

Breathing Meditation

Concentrate on your breath to calm the mind and reduce stress. Great for anxiety relief.

Guided Meditation

Follow audio instructions for relaxation and visualization. Ideal for sleep and stress management.
