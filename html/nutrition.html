Optimal Meal Timing

Breakfast (7-8 AM)

  1. High protein to start the day
  2. Complex carbs for energy
  3. Include fruits or vegetables
  4. Avoid sugary cereals
  5. Examples: Oats, Eggs, Smoothies

Lunch (12-1 PM)

  1. Balanced protein and carbs
  2. Include plenty of vegetables
  3. Moderate portion size
  4. Stay hydrated with water
  5. Examples: Salad, Grilled chicken, Rice

Dinner (7-8 PM)

  1. Lighter than lunch
  2. Focus on protein and veggies
  3. Limit heavy carbs
  4. Finish 2-3 hours before sleep
  5. Examples: Soup, Fish, Vegetables
